Mathematics

Model mathematics

1 · A team's goals ~ Poisson

We treat a team's total goals as a Poisson random variable whose single parameter λ is its expected goals. The probability of scoring exactly k:

P(X = k) = e−λ · λk / k!

2 · Double Poisson: the joint distribution

Home X ~ Poisson(λh), away Y ~ Poisson(λa). The base model assumes independence, so any scoreline's probability is the product of the two:

P(X=x, Y=y) = P(X=x) · P(Y=y)

Iterating over all (x, y) gives the scoreline matrix.

3 · Dixon-Coles low-score correction

The independence assumption is biased at low scores (it underrates 0-0, overrates 1-1). Dixon-Coles (1997) add a correlation parameter ρ and an adjustment τ that touches only four low-score cells:

τ(0,0) = 1 − λhλaρ　　τ(1,1) = 1 − ρ τ(0,1) = 1 + λhρ　　　τ(1,0) = 1 + λaρ others (x,y) = 1
P(x,y) = τ(x,y) · Poisson(x;λh) · Poisson(y;λa)

The corrected matrix is renormalized (all cells sum back to 1). ρ<0 raises 0-0/1-0/0-1 and lowers 1-1; ρ=0 reverts to plain double Poisson.

4 · From matrix to markets

Each market is a sum over the cells that satisfy it:

Home win = Σx>y　Draw = Σx=y　Away win = Σx<y Over 2.5 = Σx+y≥3　Home −1.5 = Σx−y≥2 Both score = Σx≥1 and y≥1

5 · Odds and EV

Convert American odds A to decimal d and implied probability:

d = 1 + A/100 (A>0)　or　1 + 100/|A| (A<0) implied = 100/(A+100) or |A|/(|A|+100)

Use model probability p for expected value per $1 and the edge over the odds:

EV/$1 = p · d − 1　　Edge = p − implied

The implied probabilities in one market sum to more than 100%; the excess is the bookmaker's margin (vig). Normalize them to get "fair" probabilities. Positive EV means the odds pay more than your probability estimate warrants.

User Guide

User guide

  1. (Recommended) Fit λ from data first. In ① at the top, paste recent results from the teams' league/competition (home, away, score, date) and click Fit; the app estimates each team's attack/defense and home advantage. Pick home and away, click Apply to model, and λ and ρ fill in automatically. With no data, drag the λ sliders manually — a rough start is the team's recent goals scored × the opponent's recent goals conceded ÷ league average.
  2. Set Dixon-Coles ρ. If unsure, leave it at −0.10 (a typical value in football data). To see plain double Poisson, set it to 0 and compare the 0-0 / 1-1 cells.
  3. Read the scoreline matrix. Rows are home goals, columns away goals; each cell is that score's probability, brighter = more likely, the outlined cell is the most likely score. This table holds all of the model's information.
  4. Read the markets table. "Model prob" is aggregated from the matrix. Type the bookmaker's price in the American-odds box (different every match — fill it yourself); implied probability, edge and EV/$1 compute automatically. Blank = no calculation. For a quick worked example, click "Load example · FRA vs SEN" on the left.
  5. Read the colors. Positive EV shows green with a +EV tag; negative shows red with SKIP. A positive edge means your probability exceeds the odds' implied probability — the source of any value.
  6. Use λ sensitivity to self-check. Pick a market and watch its probability as the home λ moves ±0.30. The more it swings, the more sensitive the market is to your λ and the less stable the conclusion.

Reading EV / Edge

EV/$1 is the long-run average profit or loss per $1 on this bet. +$0.05 means averaging five cents profit per bet; −$0.05 means losing five cents. Edge is the percentage-point gap: your probability minus the odds' implied probability.

Mathematics

模型数学原理

1 · 单队进球 ~ 泊松分布

假设一支球队整场进球数是泊松随机变量，唯一参数 λ 是它的预期进球数。进 k 球的概率:

P(X = k) = e−λ · λk / k!

2 · 双泊松:两队联合分布

主队 X ~ Poisson(λh)，客队 Y ~ Poisson(λa)。基础模型假设两者独立，于是任一比分的概率是两个泊松概率相乘:

P(X=x, Y=y) = P(X=x) · P(Y=y)

遍历所有 (x, y) 就得到那张比分概率矩阵。

3 · Dixon-Coles 低比分修正

独立假设在低比分上有系统偏差(低估 0-0、高估 1-1)。DC(1997)引入相关参数 ρ 和调整函数 τ,只改动四个低比分格子:

τ(0,0) = 1 − λhλaρ　　τ(1,1) = 1 − ρ τ(0,1) = 1 + λhρ　　　τ(1,0) = 1 + λaρ 其余 (x,y) = 1
P(x,y) = τ(x,y) · Poisson(x;λh) · Poisson(y;λa)

修正后矩阵会重新归一化(全部格子之和回到 1)。ρ<0 抬高 0-0/1-0/0-1、压低 1-1；ρ=0 即退回纯双泊松。

4 · 从矩阵到盘口

每个盘口都是对满足条件的格子求和:

主队胜 = Σx>y　平 = Σx=y　客胜 = Σx<y 大 2.5 = Σx+y≥3　主队 −1.5 = Σx−y≥2 双方进球 = Σx≥1 且 y≥1

5 · 赔率换算与 EV

美式赔率 A 转十进制 d 与隐含概率:

d = 1 + A/100 (A>0)　或　1 + 100/|A| (A<0) 隐含 = 100/(A+100) 或 |A|/(|A|+100)

用模型概率 p 算每 $1 的期望值,以及相对赔率的优势:

EV/$1 = p · d − 1　　Edge = p − 隐含概率

同一市场各项隐含概率之和会大于 100%,多出来的部分就是庄家的水位(vig)。要算"公平概率"可把它们归一化。EV 为正意味着赔率给的回报超过了你对真实概率的估计。

User Guide

使用说明书

  1. (推荐)先用数据拟合 λ。 在顶部「① 用历史结果拟合」里粘贴两队所在联赛/赛事的近期比赛结果(主队，客队，比分，日期)，点「拟合」，程序会估出每队攻防强度与主场优势；选好主客队后点「代入下方模型」，λ 和 ρ 自动填好。没有数据时，也可在下方手动拖 λ 滑块——粗估可用「该队近期场均进球 × 对手近期场均失球 ÷ 联赛平均」。
  2. 调 Dixon-Coles 的 ρ。 不确定就保留 −0.10(足球数据里的典型值)。想看纯双泊松的样子，把它拨到 0，对比 0-0 / 1-1 这几格的变化。
  3. 读比分概率矩阵。 行是主队进球、列是客队进球，每格是该比分的概率，颜色越亮越可能，描边那格是最可能比分。这张表包含了模型的全部信息。
  4. 读盘口表。 「模型概率」是矩阵聚合出的结果。在「美式赔率」框里填庄家挂出的赔率(每场比赛各不相同，自己填)，右侧自动算出隐含概率、Edge 和 EV/$1。框留空则不计算。想快速看一个完整例子，点左侧「载入示例 · FRA vs SEN」。
  5. 看颜色判断。 EV 为正显示绿色并标 +EV，为负显示红色并标 SKIP。Edge 为正表示你的概率高于赔率隐含，是优势的来源。
  6. 用 λ 敏感性自检。 选一个盘口，看主队 λ 上下浮动 ±0.30 时概率怎么变。变化越剧烈，说明该盘口对你的 λ 估计越敏感、结论越不稳。

怎么读 EV / Edge

EV/$1 是「这一注长期平均每 $1 赚或赔多少」。+$0.05 表示长期每注平均赚五美分，−$0.05 表示平均赔五美分。Edge 是百分点差:你的概率减去赔率隐含概率。
